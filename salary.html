Salary Range

The salary range for computer engineers varies depending on factors such as experience, education, location, and the specific industry. On average, computer engineers can expect to earn between $65,000 and $130,000 per year. With the right choices and career decisions gaining experiences and changing from workplace to workplace a large upwards salary of over $130,000 can be made.

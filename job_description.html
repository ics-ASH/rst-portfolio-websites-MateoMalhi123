Job Description and Responsibilities

Computer engineers are responsible for designing, developing, and testing computer hardware and software systems. They often work on components such as processors, memory devices, and communication interfaces. Additionally, they may be involved in optimizing system performance, troubleshooting issues, and ensuring compatibility with existing systems. Many skills are required in this sector of work. In terms of the classes needed to be taken at McGill mentioned in the education required, a wide range of courses regarding maths, programming and many more seemingly difficult classes must be taken.

Computer engineers are also responsible for creating and maintaining technology. They listen to what people need, design solutions, write code, and make sure everything works smoothly and safely. They have to keep learning about new technology so they can remain advanced in their job and help make things better for everyone.

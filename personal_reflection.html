Entrance Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement (usually around 3.0 or higher)
  • Letters of recommendation

First Year Program:

The first-year program typically includes introductory courses in computer science, mathematics, and engineering principles. Some common courses may include:

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Calculus
  • Physics
  • Introduction to Electrical Engineering
  • Programming Fundamentals

Sample of Other Requirements:

In addition to completing coursework, students may need to fulfill requirements such as:

  • Internships or co-op experiences
  • Senior design project
  • Capstone project
  • Thesis (for graduate students)

The length of time to complete a bachelor's degree in computer engineering is typically four years, although some programs may offer accelerated options or require additional time for internships or research projects.
